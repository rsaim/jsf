Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy reflects complete commitment to keep user information strictly confidential. The site may, however, use this information from time to time for purposes such as corresponding with the users for various issues. We shall duly seek permission of the users in such cases.

We are committed to use information accurate and fairly, keep it secure and up to date.

The policy will answer these questions.

We collect personal information(demographic, browsing details and personal profile data) when you create an account or edit your account information ordonate. Some of this information is a legal requirement of the Government of India, such as your postal address. Other information is used to facilitate your use of the site such as knowing your taxpayer status so that we show you options that you are eligible to make as well as amounts in your local currency.

Sakshi may place internet ‘cookies’ on your hard drive. These cookies may save data about you such as your name and the pages of the website that you viewed. You may decline, disable or delete the internet cookies while visiting the website by activating the relevant browser setting. However, doing so may affect the website functionality.

Sakshi uses the information to help us understand more about how supporters use our site and to make improvements. We also use the information for marketing purposes such as sending you our e-newsletter.

We do not collect or have access to the credit card numbers or bank account information our third-party vendorsto process online transactions.

Sakshi will not share, rent or sell your personal information to third parties.We make every effort to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100 percent secure.

We take privacy seriously and we value yours. In case you have any further questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please write to us at connect@sakshingo.org
