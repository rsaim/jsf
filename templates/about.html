Established since 2010

Jeevan stambh foundation is a group of committed volunteers based out of delhi that started as #migranttravelsupport with the objective to address the immediate needs of the stranded migrants and their families during the covid-led lockdown, which included providing them with food/ration, hygiene/medical aid and arranging travel to help the stranded migrants reach their home safely.

Soon after, we expanded the ambit of our relief work towards rebuilding lives & restoring livelihoods by connecting them to probable employers and/or by helping them start/re-start their work through small yet self-sustainable businesses.

We further expanded our work by providing relief to those affected by: floods in assam & bihar, by demolition of slum areas in delhi & fire breakout in a factory in delhi among others.
