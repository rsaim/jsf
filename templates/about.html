Established since 2020

Jeevan Stambh Foundation is a non-profit organisation working towards livelihood restoration, crisis intervention, education, health and the environment. Based in Delhi, JSF was founded by Chandrajeet Kumar Gupta, Sonam Chaturvedi, Mohd Arman Khan, Shaurya Roy, and Upamanyu Raju began as a crisis intervention mission during COVID 19.

How it started

Jeevan Stambh Foundation welcomes volunteers who wish to utilise their skills and contribute towards the betterment of several socially disadvantaged communities. Doctors, teachers or any other working professionals can reach out to us and connect over our ongoing projects that suit their domains.
