Our Causes

COVID/Lockdown Crises

Rehabilitate and restore livelihoods of migrant workers adversely affected by Covid-19 and associated lockdown in India, we have worked in the following areas with the help of partner organizations and a dedicated group of volunteers.

Fire Relief

In a massive fire near Daulatpur, Shahbad dairy in Delhi on the 16th of July, more than 80 slum dwellings were gutted. Residents lost their homes and belongings overnight.

Flood Relief

With the help of our volunteers, partner organizations and donors, we distributed essential relief goods – including ration kits, sanitary pads, mosquito nets, slippers – to flood affected regions of Bihar and Assam.

Medical Emergencies

Rescue Efforts

We helped with efforts of local NGOs and activist groups to rescue laborers working as bonded labor in brick kilns. We also further sponsored their travel to their homes. We were able to amplify these stories and arrange timely support for rescued workers.
